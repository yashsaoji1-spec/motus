Before you begin

What we collect

  • Movement data captured via your device camera
  • Exercise performance (reps, range of motion, pain level)
  • Session history shared with your assigned therapist
  • Messages between you and your therapist

How we use it

  • Your data is only shared with your connected therapist.
  • Session videos are stored securely and automatically removed after 30 days.
  • All data is encrypted in transit and stored securely.
  • Contact your therapist for any questions regarding your data.
Please check the box to confirm your consent before continuing.
